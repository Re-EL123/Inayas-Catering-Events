About Inayas

Established in 2024 in the heart of South Africa, Inayas Catering & Events stands proudly as a 100% Black-owned, BBBEE Level 1, and CSD-registered powerhouse—driven by visionary founder Hanifa Ntuli. From our first bespoke canapé platter to grand-scale gala dinners, we have rapidly become the go-to partner for clients who demand nothing short of culinary excellence and flawless execution. Our team blends time-honored African flavors with modern techniques, crafting unforgettable experiences that elevate every gathering—from intimate VIP soirées to multi-day corporate conferences. Backed by a commitment to sustainable sourcing, community empowerment, and an unyielding attention to detail, Inayas doesn’t just cater events—we architect moments that resonate, inspire trust, and leave a lasting impression. Whether you’re celebrating life’s milestones or forging new business alliances, you can be confident Inayas Catering & Events will deliver a seamless fusion of taste, style, and professionalism.
